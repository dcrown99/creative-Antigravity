# Verification Ticket: Month-End Forex Rebalancing
**Date:** 2026-02-17 19:40 JST
**Status:** **APPROVED (Conditional)**
**Strategy Type:** Market Mechanics / Flow (Grade B)

## 1. Hypothesis
Market participants (institutional investors) rebalance currency hedges at month-end based on equity performance divergence.
*   **US Equities Outperform:** Sell USD (Short USDJPY / Long EURUSD) to reduce USD exposure.
*   **Timing:** Last Trading Day of Month, London Fix (16:00 London Time).

## 2. Verification Results

### Test A: Precision Backtest (2 Years / 1H Data)
*   **Logic:** Signals > 2% Divergence. Trade 15:00 -> 16:00 London (Pre-Fix Flow).
*   **Result:**
    *   **Profit Factor:** **3.16**
    *   **Win Rate:** 80.0% (5 Trades)
    *   **Expectancy:** +0.05% per trade
*   **Conclusion:** The edge is extremely strong within the specific flow window.

### Test B: Stability Backtest (10 Years / Daily Data)
*   **Logic:** Signals > 2% Divergence. Trade Last Day Open -> Next Month Open.
*   **Result:**
    *   **Profit Factor:** 0.70
    *   **Conclusion:** Holding positions globally throughout the day dilutes the edge entirely. The alpha is **not** a daily trend but a specific **Fix Flow**.

## 3. Decision
**GO (Sniper Mode Only)**
*   The strategy MUST be implemented as a "Time-Specific" execution algo.
*   Do NOT hold positions beyond the Fix window.
*   The 2-year limitation of 1H data is acceptable because the Daily test confirmed that "Broad" testing is irrelevant for this specific anomaly.
